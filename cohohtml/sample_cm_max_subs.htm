Maximal subgroups



In this page we list all the maximal subgroups of the group G. For each maximal subgroup, we provide a link to the web page of a group isomorphic to it, a list of generators in the group G, a table describing the restriction map, and another table describing the transfer map. We also give generators of the kernel of the restriction. At the bottom of this page we describe the image of the transfers from all the maximal subgroups, as well as the essential cohomology of the group G. This information can also be accessed directly using the "Essential cohomology" link.
