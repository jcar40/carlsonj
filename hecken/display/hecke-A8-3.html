The Hecke Algebra

The Hecke algebra H of the module M is the A-endomorphism ring of M.

The dimension of H is 11 .

The dimensions of the irreducible H-modules are 1, 1, 1, 1 .

The degrees of the splitting fields are 1, 1, 1, 1 .
