PSL(2,13) Modulo 13

The Hecke algebra for the Projective Special Linear Group of dimension 2 over GF(13) with point stabilizer being the normalizer of a Sylow 13-subgroup in characteristic 13 .

The Module M

The module M is the permutation module over the prime field of chacteristic 13, having point stablilizer of order 78 The dimension of M is 14 .

The dimensions of the irreducible submodules modules are 13, 1 .

The Action Algebra

The action algebra A is the image of kG in the k-endomorphism ring of M. It's simple modules are the irreducible submodules of M.
