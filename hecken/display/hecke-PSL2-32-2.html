PSL(2,32) Modulo 2

The Hecke algebra for the Projective Special Linear Group of dimension 2 over GF(32) with point stabilizer being the normalizer of a Sylow 2-subgroup in characteristic 2 .

The Module M

The module M is the permutation module over the field of chacteristic 2, having point stablilizer of order 992 . The dimension of M is 33 .

The dimensions of the irreducible submodules modules are 32, 1 .

The module M is semi-simple. It has composition factors consisting of modules 1, 2

The Action Algebra

The action algebra A is the image of kG in the k-endomorphism ring of M. It's simple modules are the irreducible submodules of M.

The algebra A is a direct product of matrix algebras of dimension 32, 1 over field extensions of the base field of degrees 1, 1 .
