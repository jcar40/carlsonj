PSL(2,19) Modulo 19

The Hecke algebra for the Projective Special Linear Group of dimension 2 over GF(19) with point stabilizer being the normalizer of a Sylow 19-subgroup in characteristic 19 .

The Module M

The module M is the permutation module over the prime field of chacteristic 19, having point stablilizer of order 171 The dimension of M is 20 .

The dimensions of the irreducible submodules modules are 19, 1 .

The module M has radical filtration (Loewy series)
1, 2


The module M has socle filtration (socle series)
1, 2


The Action Algebra

The action algebra A is the image of kG in the k-endomorphism ring of M. It's simple modules are the irreducible submodules of M.

The dimensions of the projective modules are 19, 1 .

The cartan matrix of A is


    1, 0
    0, 1

The determinant of the Cartan matrix is 1.
